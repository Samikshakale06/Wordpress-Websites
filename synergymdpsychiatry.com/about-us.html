How I Got Started

I envisioned being a psychiatrist from the time I went to medical school. That vision and dream came to fruition while I completed psychiatry training first in India and then here in the United States. Being a psychiatrist is an integral part of me and has grown as I have over the years. As a young doctor, I believed I could make a difference in the world through my work and that belief has carried me over through my journey thus far.

Where We Are Now

Working on the inpatient psychiatric hospital units is gratifying when patients are discharged, however it is also disheartening to see the same patients readmitted due to lack of care and follow-up or support in the community.

SynergyMDPsychiatry was formed with the intent of providing treatment and support in the community.

The goal of SynergyMDPsychiatry is to assist, treat and support my patients in the community to reach their goals together.

Years of experience in Medical Services

SynergyMDPsychiatry believes in meeting each individual where they are. We offer comprehensive evaluations and individually tailored plans to suit your individual life circumstances and needs. We do not believe in a “one size fits all” treatment. For some of you this may be a recommendation for therapy, for others it may be medications or a combination of both.

Our Mission

Along with treating people and helping them understand their illness, we will strive to increase the awareness of mental health problems and the need for treatment. The most important aspect of this is to destigmatize mental illness and promote more open conversations and dialogues about it.

Our Philosophy & Vision

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), mental health is “a state of well-being in which the individual realizes his or her own abilities, can cope with the normal stresses of life, can work productively and fruitfully, and is able to make a contribution to his or her community.”

In today’s very busy, “always on the go” lifestyle, many of us may find it difficult to find this balance and state of equanimity. We would like to help you work towards that. Whether it is in your work, personal life or both.

Our process towards this goal is simple… We meet you where you are. Together, we come up with a plan keeping in mind your destination and the path you wish to take towards that goal. For some of you this may be a recommendation for therapy, for others it may be medications or a combination of both.

Our vision is to support you, educate you and finally empower you to reach your goals.
