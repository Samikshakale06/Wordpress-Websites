Welcome to Omkar CFPL

At Omkar CFPL we’re not just about logistics; we’re about boosting and streamlining operations with a touch of efficiency. Headquartered in lively Mumbai, we’ve expanded our footprint to Ahmedabad, Chennai, and Pune to ensure prompt services because, let’s face it, time matters.

Operating with a global perspective, our associates span across more than 45 countries, specializing in everything from transportation and project handling to break bulk and vessel loads. As your international freight agent, we seamlessly handle import and export, air and sea freight, ensuring smooth processes like transshipment, triangle and bypass shipments, and door-to-door deliveries. Our services go beyond the basics, covering custom clearance, consolidation, local transportation, and both bonded and non-bonded warehousing for short or long-term storage.

At OCFPL, we’re not just freight agents; we’re partners in reliable and prompt service. Our team, well-versed in project cargo handling, understands the essence of timely delivery. We’ve established robust connections with airlines and shipping lines in India, ensuring the best prices, transparent dealings, and competitive services for our customers.

What sets us apart? Our emphasis is on reliability. When you choose OCFPL, you’re not just guaranteed services; you’re assured a personalized touch that prioritizes your unique needs. Join us, and experience logistics with a human touch.
