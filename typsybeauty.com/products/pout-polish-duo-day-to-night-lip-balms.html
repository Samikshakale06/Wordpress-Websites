Description

Description

Introducing the Pout Polish AM:PM Duo — your complete lip ritual, designed for effortless beauty and deep care from morning to night.

✨ AM Tinted Balm: Start your day with our tinted Pout Polish balm – a skincare-meets-makeup multitasker. It hydrates like a lip mask, adds shine like a gloss, and tints like a dream, giving you the perfect “skincare as makeup” finish. Think glazed lips with real benefits.

🌙 PM Clear Balm: End your day with our clear, untinted balm – a powerful overnight lip treatment that heals, soothes, and repairs while you sleep. Wake up to softer, plumper, and more rejuvenated lips every morning.

Both balms are powered by science-backed skincare actives like peptides and collagen, and infused with dessert-inspired scents that make every application a moment of joy.

What makes it so goood:

  • AM:PM Lip Routine: Tinted balm for daytime glow, untinted balm for overnight repair

  • Metal Tip Applicator: Gives a refreshing, cooling experience with every swipe

  • Triple Action Formula: Hydration of a lip mask, shine of a lip glaze, nourishment of a lip treatment balm

  • Science-Backed Ingredients: Peptides and collagen hydrate, smooth fine lines, and plump lips over time

  • Non-Sticky High Shine: Delivers a radiant, glazed finish without any stickiness

  • Comfortable, Lightweight Formula: Wraps lips in non-greasy, bouncy moisture

  • Moisture Lock Technology: Provides up to 8 hours of hydration and comfort

  • Dessert-Inspired Scents: Each balm features indulgent dessert-like fragrances for a sensory treat

100% Vegan & Cruelty-free. Dermatologically Tested

How to Use

AM Tinted Balm: Press the tube gently to release the balm and glide it across your lips. Apply one layer for a sheer, juicy tint or build it up for extra color and shine — perfect for that “skincare as makeup” glow on the go.
PM Clear Balm:Apply a generous layer on clean lips before bedtime. Let the nourishing formula work overnight to repair, hydrate, and plump while you sleep.

Typsy Trick

For a fuller, more defined pout during the day, line your lips with a matching lip liner before applying the tinted balm. At night, apply the clear balm slightly outside your lip line too — this helps soften fine lines around the lips while you dream.
