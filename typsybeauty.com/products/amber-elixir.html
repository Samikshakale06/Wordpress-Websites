Description

AM Story:  

Accords - Amber, Aromatic, Sweet, Woody  

Amber AM is a sophisticated fragrance for the woman who commands attention without speaking. It blends sweet amber and saffron with warm brown sugar and toffee, creating an elegant and sensual scent. Its heart of jasmine and cedarwood adds balance, while ambergris and fir resin deepen the warmth, leaving a luxurious trail of quiet strength and refinement. A fragrance for those who exude effortless elegance. 

AM Notes

TOP: Saffron, Jasmine  

MID: Amber, Brown Sugar, Ambergris   

BASE: Toffee, Oakmoss, Ambroxan, Cedar   

  

PM Story:  

Accords - Amber, Vanilla, Leather, Spicy  

Amber PM is for the fearless, rebellious woman who owns the room. A bold blend of spicy pink pepper, saffron, and smoky leather creates a powerful, seductive scent. Sweet caramel, bourbon vanilla, and amber add warmth, while the leather adds a raw, rebellious edge. This fragrance leaves a captivating trail, perfect for the woman who demands attention and isn’t afraid to break the rules.  

PM  Notes

TOP: Caramel, Saffron, Pink Pepper  

MID: Leather, Tonka Bean, Lily of Valley  

BASE: Bourbon Vanilla, Amber, Musk

Lasting Power: 

Extrait de Parfum is the most luxurious concentration in the world of fragrances, with an oil content level of 30-40% for a richer, longer-lasting scent. Designed to evolve beautifully on your skin, it promises 12+ hours of scent that lingers like a memory.

Notes

AM Notes:  

  • Saffron: A warm and exotic note with a subtle sweetness, adding a luxurious touch to the opening.  
  • Jasmine: A bright, floral note that introduces elegance and femininity to the fragrance.  
  • Amber: A rich and resinous note that exudes warmth and depth, creating a cozy and inviting heart.  
  • Brown Sugar: A sweet, caramelized note that enhances the fragrance with a gourmand richness.  
  • Toffee: A decadent and creamy note that blends sweetness with indulgent warmth.  
  • Oakmoss: A green and earthy note that grounds the fragrance with a natural, woody texture.  
  • Cedar: Clean, dry woody note with fresh forest character  
  • Ambergris: Precious marine note with sweet, salty character   
  • Ambroxan: Long-lasting amber note that's clean, warm, and subtly mineral.  

PM Notes  

  • Caramel: A luscious, sweet note that envelops the fragrance in warmth and indulgence.  
  • Saffron: An opulent and spicy-sweet note that adds depth and richness to the opening.  
  • Leather: A bold and smoky note that adds a luxurious and sensual quality to the heart.  
  • Tonka Bean: A sweet and creamy note with hints of almond and vanilla, creating warmth and depth.  
  • Bourbon Vanilla: A creamy and boozy vanilla note that adds sweetness and an elegant warmth to the base.  
  • Amber: A resinous and golden note that enhances the richness and allure of the fragrance.  
  • Musk: Soft, skin-like warmth that adds depth and subtle sensuality.  
  •  Pink Pepper: Light, spicy note with a fresh, rosy character   
  • Lily of the Valley: Fresh, dewy floral with a clean, spring-like fragrance
